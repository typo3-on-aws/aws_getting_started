Backend Login

The backend of TYPO3 CMS is the administration interface, which allows you to build and maintain your website. The access credentials provided give you full administrator access to the system.

Before you continue, make sure you retrieved the access details (admin user and password) as described!

In order to access the backend of TYPO3 CMS, simply append /typo3 at the end of the URL. Currently, the backend login is available at: http://{hostname}/typo3.
